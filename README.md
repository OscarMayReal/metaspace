# MetaSpace

MetaSpace is a social platform, a fun place to hang out with friends while you talk.

note: this is a beta version, there are a few bugs and sill some placeholders left over

## How to use

1. go to https://metaspace.quntem.co.uk
2. sign in with your Quntem account
3. click on "Start a Space"
4. click on "Empty Space" (or "Import from File" to import a space from a file)
5. click on "Join"
6. share the code with your friends

## How to join a space

1. go to https://metaspace.quntem.co.uk
2. sign in with your Quntem account
3. click on "Join a Space"
4. enter the code of the space you want to join
5. click on "Join"
